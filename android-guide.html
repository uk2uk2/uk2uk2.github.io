Guide for Android

Follow these steps to clear cache and cookies on your Android device:

  1. Open the Chrome app on your Android device.
  2. Tap the three dots in the upper right corner to open the menu.
  3. Select "History" and then "Clear browsing data".
  4. Choose a time range and select "Cookies and site data" and "Cached images and files".
  5. Tap "Clear data" to confirm.
